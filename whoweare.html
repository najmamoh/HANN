As the chair of the board and a co-founder of Hanuun University, it is both an honor and a privilege to welcome you to our university. Whether you are a new member of our community or a familiar face, I want to extend my warmest greetings to each of you. At Hanuun University, we pride ourselves on being more than just an institution of higher learning. We are a community that values diversity, fosters innovation, and is committed to making a meaningful impact on the world. Our mission is to empower you to achieve your academic, personal, and professional goals.

To our students, you are the heart of our university. Your passion for learning, creativity, and determination are what drive us forward. I encourage you to engage deeply in your studies, seek out new experiences, and take advantage of the resources and support systems available to you. Your time here is an opportunity to grow not only academically but also personally and socially. To our faculty and staff, thank you for your dedication and hard work. Your commitment to excellence in teaching, research, and service is what can make Hanuun University a leader in higher education. Together, we will continue to create a supportive and dynamic environment where everyone can thrive.

As we move forward, let us embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead with a spirit of collaboration and a shared commitment to the values that define our university. Thank you for being a part of the Hanuun University family. I look forward to the incredible achievements we will accomplish together. With warm regards, Dr. Yacquub Aw Adan Abdi MD, PhD Chair of the Board of Hanuun University
