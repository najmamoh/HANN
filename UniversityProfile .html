Hanuun University

A Source of Understanding Society and Inspiring Positive Change

University Profile

Hanuun University is a distinctive educational institution in Somaliland, dedicated to addressing the nation's critical social and behavioral challenges. Through its specialized programs in psychiatry, psychology, sociology, and Peace and Conflict Studies, the university responds to a significant demand for these fields, which are not widely available elsewhere in the region.

Primarily focused on postgraduate education, Hanuun University offers certificates, diplomas, and master's degrees, with plans to introduce PhD programs in the future. Given the scarcity of professors in these disciplines, the university delivers most of its courses online, ensuring that education remains accessible to students across Somaliland.

Vision

To be a premier center of excellence in social and behavioral sciences, globally recognized for advancing knowledge, fostering critical thinking, and inspiring positive societal change.

Mission

Hanuun University is dedicated to advancing the understanding of human societies, cultures, and behaviors through rigorous research, innovative teaching, and community engagement. We strive to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to address complex societal challenges, promote inclusivity and diversity, and contribute to the betterment of our global community.

Core Values

Committed to fostering a culture of academic rigour, critical thinking, and continuous learning among its students and faculty.

Commitments of Hanuun University

Hanuun University integrates Somaliland’s culture and Islamic values into all its teaching and research programs, ensuring that local contexts and cultural influences are respected.

Skill-Based Learning

Adopting modern, interactive teaching methods that encourage students to engage deeply with the subject matter, dedicating 60% of teaching time to practical exercises.

Effective Communication Skills

Emphasizing the development of strong communication skills across all programs to ensure students' success in the labor market.

Research and Societal Impact

Focusing on research that contributes to societal development, with a strong emphasis on building research capacity among students.

Quality Assurances

1

Standard Curriculum

Hanuun University’s curriculum design procedure involves forming a committee, setting learning objectives, selecting appropriate content, organizing materials, and developing assessments. It ensures alignment with educational standards, accommodates diverse learning styles, and integrates continuous evaluation. Teachers implement the curriculum, monitor progress, and make adjustments to meet student needs, ensuring a comprehensive educational experience.

2

Students Admission Criteria

Student selection is vital for academic success, as they are key to applying the curriculum. The university requires a minimum 70% overall score, an entry test, and an interview. These criteria ensure that admissions choose candidates with strong potential to succeed in their chosen program.

3

Criteria of Academic Staff

Teachers at Hanuun University must hold a PhD in their teaching field and at least the rank of assistant professor. This requirement ensures they can effectively deliver curriculum content, foster a positive learning environment, and support students' academic and personal growth, meeting the university's high standards for education.

Mode of Delivery

Face-to-Face

Face-to-face learning is essential in health sciences education because it provides students with hands-on experience and direct interaction, which are critical for developing practical skills and clinical competence.

This method allows students to engage in real-world scenarios, practice procedures, and receive immediate feedback from instructors. It also enhances the development of communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills, which are vital in healthcare settings.

Additionally, face-to-face learning fosters a deeper understanding of patient care, as students observe and interact with patients, building empathy and professional behavior. These experiences are irreplaceable in preparing students for the demands of healthcare practice.

Online

There is a critical need to produce qualified professionals capable of addressing Somaliland's pressing social issues. To achieve this, highly competent and experienced instructors are essential. However, such professionals are scarce or nonexistent in Somaliland, requiring expertise from abroad.

Thanks to advances in digital technology and eLearning, these professionals can teach remotely through synchronous learning, which allows real-time interaction between instructors and students via video conferencing, live chats, or webinars.

Final exams are conducted face-to-face at pre-certified institutions in Somaliland or nearby regions. This mode of learning, increasingly adopted by prestigious institutions worldwide, is cost-effective and reaches students across diverse locations.

Community Engagement

Free Public Lectures

Hanuun University offers free public lectures on social issues, featuring faculty and global experts discussing topics like mental health, conflict resolution, and sustainability to promote community knowledge and understanding.

Free Seminars and Workshops

Hanuun University’s free online activities, including webinars and virtual workshops, offer valuable educational content on mental health and globalization, extending access to remote and underserved communities globally.

Scholarships for Disadvantaged Groups

Scholarships for disadvantaged groups enabling access to education for those facing economic or social barriers, empowering them to pursue academic and professional opportunities.

Free Short Courses

Hanuun University offers free, accessible short courses on topics like conflict resolution, psychological first aid, and sustainability, empowering community members with practical skills to drive positive societal change.
