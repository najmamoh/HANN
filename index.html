ABOUT US

Hanuun University is a distinguished institution of higher learning established in the heart of Somaliland, dedicated to addressing the critical needs of our society through comprehensive educational programs. Our university is renowned for its focus on essential disciplines, including Psychiatry, Psychology, Sociology, and Peace and Conflict Studies. These fields are integral to understanding and tackling the myriad challenges faced by Somaliland society, and we are committed to providing our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to inspire positive change within our community and beyond.
